The Courses
SLO County Golf
Seven courses within 40 min — pick your rounds
Hunter Ranch
18 · 40 min N
Best course in SLO County. 4.5 stars Golf Digest. Rolling oak-studded fairways through wine country, no houses on the course, pristine greens. Paso Robles. The signature experience.
yelp
Morro Bay Golf Course
18 · 25 min W
Most scenic muni in California. Ocean views from every hole, Morro Rock as your backdrop. Voted "Best Golf Course" repeatedly by local papers. Great intermediate-level play.
yelp
Avila Beach Golf Resort
18 · 15 min S
Classic resort layout with estuary and ocean views. 4.3 stars, 1,300+ reviews. Good mix of hole lengths and strategic variety. Reliable conditioning. Closest full 18 to downtown.
yelp
Cypress Ridge
18 · 20 min S
Peter Jacobsen design in Arroyo Grande. Hilly routing, elevated tees and greens, ocean views. 4.1 stars. More challenging — rewards thoughtful club selection. Multiple tee placements for intermediate play.
yelp
Chalk Mountain
18 · 20 min N
Hilly, challenging 18 in Atascadero. Well-maintained, comfortable vibe. Good value and rarely crowded. Solid intermediate option.
yelp
Sea Pines Golf Resort
9 · 20 min W
Executive 9-hole in Los Osos. 4.4 stars, hidden gem. Tree lines, subtle slopes, ocean-close. Great for a quick round or warm-up day. Relaxed, thoughtful layout.
yelp
Dairy Creek
9 · 5 min
City-run 9-hole, walking distance from downtown. 4.5 stars, local favorite. Mix of par 3, 4, and 5. Flat, forgiving — good quick-play option or afternoon wind-down round.
yelp
Pismo Beach Golf
9 · 15 min S
Par-3 executive course. 4.4 stars. Balanced layout, approachable but technical. Good ocean views. Rewards mid-range accuracy. Quick, pleasant round.
yelp
